Life

With over three decades of experience in the field of technology, and nearly 30 years spent working in the corporate world, I have had the opportunity to witness and contribute to the rapid evolution of this industry.

If you're interested in exploring my recent professional history, I invite you to take a look at my LinkedIn profile, where you can see a detailed overview of the past decade of my career. Additionally, you can find information about some of my academic achievements on Credly, where I have documented part of my educational journey.

Reflecting back on my early days, I remember starting out in a world where Windows 3.11 was the operating system of choice, CorelDraw 3.0 was the graphics software du jour, and I even remember using the now-defunct PageMaker. However, my interest in technology started much earlier, when I received my first computer from my parents. Although it was a simple machine that only ran MS-DOS, it ignited a passion in me that has continued to grow and thrive ever since.

Having studied electronics in high school, I developed a deep and abiding love for all things tech-related. Over the years, I have gained a wealth of experience and knowledge, and I am always eager to explore new trends and technologies in this rapidly evolving field.
